Lodging

Barcelona (BCN)

Lodging is ample in Barcelona, but can be pricey. You can avoid spending lots of money by:

  • Bypassing Barcelona entirely by staying in SCQ any day before the 25th or after the 28th.
  • Arranging with us (Joe/Coloma) to use our 2 bedroom apartment (already taken the night of the 24th, but otherwise available).

Alternatively, you can get a hotel or AirBnB in Barcelona.

Palamós

For simplicity's sake, everyone should make their own reservations for Palamós. Hotels are an option, but it'll be cheaper and more enjoyable to group together families and get house/apartments through AirBnB.

Let's strive to all stay near each other. We suggest the following area

Longitude: 3.114530 to 3.135215

Latitude: 41.848384 to 41.852731

Santa Coloma de Queralt (SCQ)

Given that this is a small town with no hotels and very few AirBnBs, we (Coloma/Joe) will arrange housing unless you prefer to do so yourself.
