Lodging

Barcelona (BCN)

Depending on when you arrive/depart, and your preference for / aversion to cities, you may want to spend a night or more in Barcelona. Lodging is ample in Barcelona, but can be pricey. You can avoid spending lots of money by:

  • Bypassing Barcelona entirely by staying in SCQ any day before the 25th or after the 28th.
  • Arranging with us (Joe/Coloma) to use our 2 bedroom apartment (already taken the night of the 24th, but otherwise available).
  • Arranging with us (Joe/Coloma) to use friends' apartments.

Alternatively, if you want to do your own thing, you can get a hotel or AirBnB in Barcelona.

Palamós

Palamós is about 1 hour 20 minutes from Barcelona. It was the Brew family reunion site even before PML.

We are currently in the process of making decisions regarding lodging in Palamós. We will likely lock down two large places with enough capacity to hold most (all?) of the attendees. Hold off on reserving anything.

Santa Coloma de Queralt (SCQ)

SCQ is 1 hour from Barcelona / 2 hours from Palamós.

Given that this is a small town with no hotels and very few AirBnBs, we (Coloma/Joe) will arrange housing unless you prefer to do so yourself.
